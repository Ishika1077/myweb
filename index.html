About me

Hi! I am Ishika Sharma, and I am currently learning UI/UX and graphic design. I have a passion for creativity and love designing digital experiences that are both beautiful and easy to use. As I go through this learning journey, I am gaining skills in UI/UX design, wireframing, prototyping, and graphic design using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Photoshop. I enjoy exploring how design can solve problems and improve user experiences. Every day, I learn something new and apply my knowledge to real-world projects. My goal is to create designs that are simple, functional, and visually appealing. In my free time, I love sketching, photography, and exploring new design trends. Traveling and experiencing different cultures also inspire my creativity. I am excited to keep learning and growing in this field. If you would like to connect or collaborate, feel free to reach out!
